we're glad you're here

The ProClimb program is tailor made for elite athletes and climbers who wish to develop into being elite athletes. After building the fundamental strength and movement basics, one can wish to progress further and make a forray into the world of competition climbing. Climbers are assessed and plans are put in place to work on weaknesses and build on strengths. This program is made for the ones with an intention to compete and climb with the biggest names in the climbing world. To achieve the elite levels of climbing form and mental strength, the participants will be required to commit to the program for a period of 6 months.

Classes for Beginner

Are you prepared to dive deep and fully commit to learning? Our Beginner Rock Climbing Course offers a comprehensive journey through all the fundamentals. Over the span of four weeks, you'll experience our top beginner classes condensed into one comprehensive course.

  • Intro to Roped Climbing Class where you get hands on experience on how to rope climb and belay.
  • Intro to Bouldering where you learn the basics on how to boulder, fall and more
  • Intro to Technique where you will learn the important foundational movements that will help in your climbing journey
  • Enjoy an energetic, yet intimate class setting (4:1 ratio)
  • Intro to Technique Lab where you can practice everything you learned with the feedback of your instructor
